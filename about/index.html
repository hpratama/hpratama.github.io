About

January 6, 2023 · 1 min · Hadyan Pratama

I am a Machine Learning Geoscientist and Technical & Business Development Specialist at dGB Earth Science, based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, serving the Asia Pacific region. My work involves developing and deploying machine learning models to tackle geoscience challenges, implementing business strategies, conducting market research, and building relationships with universities across the region.

Prior to joining dGB Earth Sciences, I earned an MSc by research in Petroleum Geoscience Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS through the Graduate Research Assistance scholarship program. My thesis, in collaboration with PETRONAS Research Sdn Bhd, focused on Automated Geological Feature Interpretation using Semi-Supervised Learning. My research interests revolve around applying Artificial Intelligence to geoscience, particularly in seismic and well log data analysis.

In addition to my full-time role, I provide machine learning consultancy, helping over 100 clients with Data Science and Python solutions. My technical expertise includes Python, MATLAB, and PyQt5. I also enjoy giving back to the community by sharing my expertise as a mentor and speaker through webinars, workshops, and podcasts.
